Description

Enter a neon-lit futuristic city where every choice shapes your destiny. Cyber Legends is a massive open-world action RPG set in a dynamic, living metropolis, where your decisions influence the story, alliances, and the city itself.

Customize your character, explore sprawling districts, engage in high-speed chases, hack into corporate networks, and uncover secrets buried deep in the urban labyrinth. With branching quests, a deep loot system, and multiplayer co-op, every playthrough is unique.

Features

  • Explore a vast, detailed open world with day-night cycles and dynamic weather
  • Customize your character with hundreds of outfits, cyberware, and weapons
  • Engage in fast-paced combat with firearms, melee, and hacking abilities
  • Shape the story through meaningful choices and moral dilemmas
  • Team up with friends in seamless online co-op
